Eric Boucher

President | Co-Creative Director | Associate

Eric, visionary founder of ANTRO, is a creator who truly understands the stakes and excels at finding ideas and solutions. Eric developed his versatility in various strategic and creative roles over his 12 years in advertising and marketing communications. His talents for innovation and creation make him a natural problem solver, whether the issues are commercial, cultural or social. His experience in traditional advertising and Web design agencies convinced him of the need, via ANTRO, of a new approach to creativity. Eric is a team player who believes that success can only be achieved and shared when everyone is allowed to reach their full potential.
